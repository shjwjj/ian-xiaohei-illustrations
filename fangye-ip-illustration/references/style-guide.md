# Fangye IP Illustration Style Guide

## Brand Role

Fangye is a young health-culture account for working adults. The visual tone should feel like a quiet note from a friend: warm, concrete, not cute for cuteness' sake, and never instructional in a classroom way.

The deer and hamster are brand IP images. They should be visible in illustrations, but article body text should not make them speak, teach, or interact with users as named characters.

## Visual DNA

- Warm watercolor or soft hand-drawn look.
- Off-white or very light warm background.
- Large blank space; the main subject can occupy only half the frame.
- Gentle Fangye palette: sage green, warm beige, soft brown, pale orange accent.
- Thin hand-drawn outline, soft texture, minimal shadow.
- Modern life scenes: sofa, bedside lamp, office bag, phone, mug, window, notebook.
- Modern clothing: pajamas, cardigan, hoodie, simple shirt, office-casual layers.

## IP Usage

Use one IP when the idea is about one person's state or action. Use two IPs only when contrast, companionship, or before/after is important.

Prefer:

- deer lying in bed and checking the phone with a small frown
- hamster writing a three-line note next to a dim lamp
- deer opening curtains in loose home clothes
- hamster stretching beside a desk after work

Avoid:

- IP as presenter, teacher, doctor, or narrator
- old-style robes unless the user specifically asks for guofeng
- complex scenic backgrounds
- corner decorations, floating flowers, ornamental borders
- props that do not help the action

## Image Text

Image text should be shorter than article text and easier than a subtitle.

Prefer concrete daily wording:

- `躺下了，又摸回来`
- `明天的事还在脑子里转。`
- `那杯咖啡早一点`
- `屋里暗一点`
- `把明天写三行`
- `试三晚，选两件就行`

Avoid AI/copywriting flavor:

- `把研究翻译成行动`
- `方也版做法`
- `递信号`
- `不是 A，而是 B`
- `核心逻辑`
- `底层机制`
- `真正有用`
- `闭环`

## Composition Rules

- One image, one job.
- If the paragraph already explains enough, do not add a diagram.
- If the reader needs to feel the situation, draw a scene.
- If the reader needs to understand sequence, render a step card.
- If exact Chinese text matters, use deterministic rendering, not model-generated text.
- For WeChat, verify on a narrow preview. Text that looks fine on desktop can be too small in the article column.

## Quality Gate

Reject or revise if:

- the IP is only a sticker in the corner
- the picture looks like a poster, PPT, or courseware
- clothing feels ceremonial or old-fashioned without reason
- background complexity competes with the main action
- Chinese labels are wrong, too long, or too clever
- the image repeats the paragraph without adding emotion or clarity
